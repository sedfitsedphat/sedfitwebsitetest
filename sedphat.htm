SEDPHAT 

the Sedimentation and Polymer Hydrodynamic Analysis Tool 

is an extension of Sedfit for the global analysis of several sedimentation velocity, sedimentation equilibrium, and/or dynamic light scattering experiments.

For example, it includes more general interaction models, more powerful distribution models, and is fully capable for multi-wavelength sedimentation equilibrium analysis of homogeneous or heterogeneous interactions.
