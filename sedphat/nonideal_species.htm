Single non-ideal species

This model is an extension for global modeling of the Sedfit model for non-ideal sedimentation.  It considers both concentration sedimentation and concentration-dependent diffusion constants. See the corresponding Sedfit website for details.   It is described also in detail in 

Solovyova, A., P. Schuck, L. Costenaro, C. Ebel (2001) Non-ideality by sedimentation velocity of halophilic malate dehydrogenase in complex solvents.  Biophys. J. 81:1868-1880

 

 

In Sedphat it is implemented for sedimentation velocity data, only.
