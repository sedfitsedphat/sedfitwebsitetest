Discrete distribution

This model is very much like the species with mass conservation model, but it does not offer the possibility to constrain sub-populations of different species. It uses mass conservation constraints for sedimentation equilibrium data.

 

 

 

Internally, this model is implemented different from the species analysis models.  It is based on the structures for continuous distributions.
